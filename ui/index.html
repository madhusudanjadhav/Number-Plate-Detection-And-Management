Overview

License Plate recognition is one of the techniques used for vehicle identification purposes. The sole intention of this project is to find the most efficient way to recognize the registration information from the digital image. This process usually comprises of three steps. First step is the license plate localization, regardless of the license-plate size and orientation. The second step is the segmentation of the characters and last step is the recognition of the characters from the license plate. Thus, this project uncovers the fundamental idea of various algorithms required to accomplish character recognition from the license plate during Template Matching.
